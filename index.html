HOW DO WE DO IT SO PERFECT?

PREPARE

Event specialist will be assigned to collect all the details and particulars about the event. Client requirement checklist will be prepared at this stage.

ASSIGN

Our event specialist will prepare the technical requirement, budget and other planning. According to the requirement Photographer/s will be assigned

PERFORM

Specialist will provide checklist to the team. Assigned team will complete the project as per the requirement and will ensure the checklist criteria's are met

CLOSE

Our editing and mixing team will collect the raw data. Project will be edited and finished as per requirement. Finished project files will be made available on our platform to clients
